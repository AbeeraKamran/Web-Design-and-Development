Presentation Tips

Try looking at individuals rather than scanning the group. Since you are using a computer, you never need to look at the screen behind you. While you’re maintaining great eye contact, don’t forget to smile as well. Confidence is key to delivering a great presentation.

Fonts communicate subtle messages, which is why you should choose fonts deliberately. Use the same font set throughout your presentation, and use no more than two complementary fonts (e.g., Arial and Arial Bold). This results in visual cohesion.
