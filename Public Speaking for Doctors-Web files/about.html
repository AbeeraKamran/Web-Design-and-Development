WHO WE ARE

Public Speaking for Doctors is run by doctors Zishan Sheikh, Afroze Khan (MRCS), and Shahnawaz Khan (MRCS). Together they have spoken at national and international conferences, presented at local and regional meetings, as well as lectured to medical students at the University of London. WHY WE DO IT

Realising, the importance of doctors as speakers, they set out to create a bespoke approach to teaching and training doctors in public speaking. Our courses are delivered in conjunction with a variety of external speakers and include supportive learning materials for reference. and body language that good speakers use to deliver their presentation.
