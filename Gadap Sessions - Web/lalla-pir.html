This is Lalla Pir. The name carries forward the memory/history of a man called Lalla, who came here from Kerman (Iran) a very long time ago. He dug a well, found abundant water and decided to stay in Pahwaro. Over the centuries, many others like Lalla have journeyed and inhabited these mountains.  Its lands are marked with histories of place-making.
