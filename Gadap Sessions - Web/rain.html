Thango was name of a man. This place is to the west of Chura waro. Here water would always be present. When sometimes water would finish, Thango would dig for 3-4 feet and water would appear. For the future, he prepared a 10 feet deep well, which had water till recently but now Bahria has taken over that land. 1000s of livestock would drink water from there. Thango loved his livestock, cared for them, was very hardworking. he belonged to Gabol clan. The place where water is available naturally is called chakhor in balochi, hence this place came to be known as thango chakhor?
