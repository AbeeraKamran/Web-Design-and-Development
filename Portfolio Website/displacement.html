I would always dream of my house.

  • Publication

'I would always dream of my house' brings together stories of displacement experienced by refugees in the wake of Partition 1947 and World War II. This publication was designed to accompany an audio-walk created by Shahana Rajani and Sonya Schroenberger. Held simulatenously in Karachi and Berlin the walk challenges state narratives of these traumas and is a poignant meditation on home and homelessness.

The booklet is a bi-lingual work in both English and Urdu.
