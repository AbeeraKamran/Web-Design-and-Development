Exhausted Geographies

  • Publication

'Exhausted Geographies' is a collection of cross disciplinary essays and map that radically engages with the poltiics of representation and map making.

The layout of each booklet takes its cues from the tone and nature of each essay. This way each booklet hosts a unique design but is backgrounded with a coherent identity designed for this collection of booklets.
