BEYOND WALLS

An hour long ‘sit in’ on the wall of Benazir Bhutto park, Karachi, Pakistan.

In response to Karachi’s increasingly privatized, securitized and segregated public spaces, the Tentative Collective organized a group sit in on (top of) the boundary wall around Shaheed Benazir Bhutto Park. This is one the city’s most enjoyed parks that has suffered dis-use and reduced visits because of the newly introduced gated entry. Some 50 people from different parts of the city including the nearby irregular housing community joined us at 4:00 pm on Sunday December 18, 2011, to drink chai and ‘hang out’ in an unusual public gathering place. Our action coincided with the International Migrants Day and was documented and tracked by Immigrant Movement International led by artist Tania Bruguera in Queens, New York.
