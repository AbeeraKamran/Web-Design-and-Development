Drawing on a network of new and returning collaborators, Different Skies provides a platform for bold experiments in criticism, short fiction, poetry and reportage. We welcome submissions and proposals at any time.

Our regular writers’ meetings are open to potential contributors, running alongside a series of live reading events. Get in touch if you’d like to read.

Join our mailing list to hear about upcoming events, calls for submissions and latest releases.
